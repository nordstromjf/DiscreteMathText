Activity 10.3.3.

In this activity we explore graphs with \(n\) edges and \(n-1\) edges more generally.

(a)

Draw a graph with 7 vertices and 6 edges that is not a tree.

(b)

Draw a connected graph with 7 vertices and 6 edges that is not a tree.

(c)

Draw a graph with 7 vertices and 6 edges that is not connected and is not a tree.

(d)

Do you think any of the tasks in (a), (b), and (c) are impossible?

(e)

Form a conjecture about graphs with \(n\) vertices and \(n-1\) edges.
