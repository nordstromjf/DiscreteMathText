Determine whether the following statements are true or false.
  1. \(\displaystyle 3\in \{1, 2, 3\}\)
  2. \(\displaystyle 1\subseteq \{1, 2, 3\}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \{2\}\in \{1, 2, 3\}\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \{3\}\in \{\{1\}, \{2\}, \{3\}\}\)
  5. \(\displaystyle 1\in \{1\}\)
  6. \(\displaystyle \{2\}\subseteq \{\{1\}, \{2\}, \{3\}\}\)
  7. \(\displaystyle \{1\}\subseteq \{1, 2, 3\}\)
  8. \(\displaystyle 1\in \{\{1\}, \{2\}, \{3\}\}\)
  9. \(\displaystyle \{1\}\subseteq \{1, \{2\}\}\)
  10. \(\displaystyle \{1\}\subseteq \{1\}\)
