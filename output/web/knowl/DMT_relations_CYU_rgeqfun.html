    Let \(A=\{1, 2, 3\}, B=\{2, 4, 6, 8\}\text{.}\) True or false: the relation on \(A\times B\) given by
    \begin{equation*} a R b \Leftrightarrow a\geq b \end{equation*}
    is a function from \(A\) to \(B\text{.}\)

  • True.

  • 1 does not map to anything in \(B\text{.}\)

  • False.

  • 1 does not map to anything in \(B\text{.}\)
