Activity 1.3.1.

Let \(A=\{1, 2, 3, 4, 5\}\) and \(B=\{0, 2, 4\}\text{.}\) Let \(R\) be the relation from \(A\) to \(B\) where \((x, y)\in R\) if \(x=y\text{.}\)

(a)

List the elements of \(R\text{.}\)

(b)

Draw the arrow diagram for \(R\text{.}\)

(c)

Is the relation \(R\) a function? Why or why not?
