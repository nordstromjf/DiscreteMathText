Method of Proof by Contrapositive.

  1. Write the statement to be proved in the form \(\forall x\in D\text{,}\) if \(P(x)\) then \(Q(x)\text{.}\)
  2. Write the contrapositive of the statement: \(\forall x\in D\text{,}\) if \(\sim Q(x)\) then \(\sim P(x)\text{.}\)
  3. Prove the contrapositive directly: assume \(\sim Q(x)\text{,}\) show \(\sim P(x)\text{.}\)
