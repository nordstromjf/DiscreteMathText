Reading Question 1.

    Let \(A=\{2, 4, 6, 8\}, B=\{3, 4\}, C=\{1, 2, 4\}, D=\{0, 1\}\)
    Match each set with its elements.
  • \(A\cap C\)
  • \(\{2, 4\}\)
  • \(B\cap D\)
  • \(\emptyset\)
  • \(A\cup C\)
  • \(\{1, 2, 4, 6, 8\}\)
  • \(B\cup D\)
  • \(\{0, 1, 3, 4\}\)
  • \(A-C\)
  • \(\{6, 8\}\)
  • \(B-D\)
  • \(\{3, 4\}\)
  • \(B\times D\)
  • \(\{(3, 0), (3, 1), (4, 0), (4, 1)\}\)
  • \(B\times B\)
  • \(\{(3, 3), (3, 4), (4, 4), (4, 3)\}\)
