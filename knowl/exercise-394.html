  1. Find \(T(1)\text{.}\)
  2. Find \(T(15)\text{.}\)
  3. Find \(T(17)\text{.}\)
  4. Find \(T(5)\text{.}\)
  5. Find \(T(18)\text{.}\)
  6. Find \(T(21)\text{.}\)
