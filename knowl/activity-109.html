Activity 6.1.2.

Let \(A_i=\{1, 2, 3, \ldots i\}\text{.}\)

(a)

Find \(A_1, A_2, A_5,\) and \(A_n\text{.}\)

(b)

Find \(\bigcup_{i=1}^{5}A_i\text{.}\)

(c)

Find \(\bigcap_{i=1}^{5}A_i\text{.}\)

(d)

Find \(\bigcup_{i=1}^{\infty}A_i\text{.}\)

(e)

Find \(\bigcap_{i=1}^{\infty}A_i\text{.}\)
